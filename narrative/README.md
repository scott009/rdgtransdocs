# Narrative Layer

**Purpose:** Context, motivation, and high-level direction for the rdgtrans project.

## What Belongs Here

- Project motivation and why it exists
- High-level context and background
- Roadmaps and exploratory ideas
- Informal reasoning and discussion documents
- Non-authoritative guidance and orientation

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- System specifications or definitions
- Operational procedures or governance
- Deterministic task instructions
- Files that define system behavior

## Relationship to Other Layers

- **Specification:** Defines *what the system is and does*
- **Operations:** Defines *how work is performed and coordinated*
- **Narrative:** Explains *why the system exists and what it intends to accomplish*

Narrative documents are non-authoritative. They provide context and motivation, not guarantees or system behavior definitions.
